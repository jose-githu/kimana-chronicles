About Quickshelf

Revolutionizing supply chain management in Kenya by connecting businesses with reliable vendors through our innovative platform.

Our Mission & Vision

We're building the supply chain superhighway for Kenya's entrepreneurs—from small retailers to hardware stores in Kimana Town, Oloitokitok. Our mission is simple: make procurement seamless, efficient, and accessible for businesses of all sizes.

We envision revolutionizing commerce in rural Kenya by making every local shop as efficient as a tech startup in Nairobi. Great business tools shouldn't be a city privilege—they should empower every entrepreneur, everywhere.

With 99% on-time delivery, same-day service availability, and 24/7 support, we ensure your business never runs out of essential supplies.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Quickshelf

How do I place an order?
Simply create a customer account, browse our catalogue, add items to your cart, and checkout. You can pay via M-Pesa, bank transfer, or cash on delivery. Orders placed before 2PM qualify for same-day delivery within Kimana.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept M-Pesa (most popular), bank transfers, and cash on delivery for local orders. For businesses, we offer flexible payment terms including 30-day credit lines for verified customers.
How can I become a vendor?
Click "Create Account," select "Vendor," and fill in your business details. We'll review and approve your account within 48 hours. No upfront fees—we only take a small commission on successful orders.
What areas do you deliver to?
We primarily serve Kimana Town and surrounding areas within Oloitokitok Sub-County. Our delivery network covers local shops, schools, and businesses within a 15km radius.
How do I track my order?
Every order gets a unique tracking ID. You can track your order in real-time through your customer dashboard or by calling our support line.
